Our Story

from Dinner Dates to Homemade Plates

@tsunam.eats was created in 2024 by a Drexel college couple. The name nods to their combined names and to the beginning of the word tsunami—a playful merge with “eats.”

Since then, every meal they make together has been posted on Instagram. With the account’s second anniversary approaching, they decided it was time to share the recipes—each one made with love.

We hope you enjoy dishes that have fueled years of amazing memories.
