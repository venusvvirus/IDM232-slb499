Kale & Ricotta Quiche

With Romaine, Apple, & Almond Salad

Ingredients
6
Prep Time
15 mins
Cook Time
25 mins
Total Time
40 mins

This savory quiche combines sautéed kale, creamy ricotta, and fresh eggs in a flaky crust—paired with a crisp salad of romaine and apple. Simple, elegant, and perfect for brunch or weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 4 large pasture-raised eggs
  • 1 cup whole-milk ricotta cheese
  • 2 packed cups chopped kale
  • 1 pie crust (blind baked or ready-to-bake)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg, 1/4 tsp paprika
  • Salt & black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 2 cups chopped romaine, 1 apple thinly sliced, handful almonds

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Preheat the oven to 375°F. Wash and dry the fresh produce. Peel and roughly chop the garlic. Thinly slice the apple. Coarsely chop the almonds (or leave whole). Chop the kale; discard stems.

Step 2: Cook the Kale

Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds. Add kale, salt, and pepper; sauté 3–4 minutes until wilted and tender. Remove from heat and cool slightly.

Step 3: Mix the Filling

In a bowl, whisk eggs. Stir in ricotta, mustard, nutmeg, paprika, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Fold in the cooled kale until just combined.

Step 4: Assemble & Bake

Place the pie crust on a sheet pan. Pour in the filling. Bake at 375°F for 25–30 minutes, until the center is just set and the top is lightly browned. Cool 5 minutes before slicing.

Step 5: Make the Salad

Toss romaine, apple, and almonds with olive oil, lemon, salt, and pepper. Adjust to taste.

Step 6: Serve

Slice the quiche and serve warm with the crisp salad. Enjoy!
